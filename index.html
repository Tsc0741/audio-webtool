About This Project

AcoustiLearn is a collection of interactive web tools designed to make acoustic education more engaging and accessible. Whether you're a student studying physics, a music enthusiast exploring sound synthesis, or an educator looking for interactive teaching aids, these tools provide hands-on experience with fundamental acoustic concepts.

The project focuses on making complex acoustic phenomena understandable through visualization and experimentation. Each tool is designed with educational principles in mind, providing immediate feedback and encouraging exploration.

Educational Applications

These tools are perfect for:

  • Physics courses covering wave mechanics and acoustics
  • Music theory and composition classes
  • Audio engineering and sound design programs
  • Self-directed learning and exploration
